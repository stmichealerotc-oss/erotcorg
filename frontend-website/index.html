About Us

Established in 2015, Saint Michael Eritrean Orthodox Tewa·∏•do Church in Western Australia serves the Eritrean Orthodox Tewa·∏•do community in WA. We are dedicated to faith, worship, spiritual integrity, and community support, rooted in the ancient Orthodox Christian tradition.

Our History

Saint Michael Eritrean Orthodox Tewa·∏•do Church was established to serve the spiritual needs of the growing Eritrean community in Western Australia. Founded as a registered religious organization, the church provides essential spiritual services, pastoral care, and community support while preserving the Orthodox Tewa·∏•do faith and heritage.

Our Mission

Saint Michael Eritrean Orthodox Tewa·∏•do Church in Western Australia Inc. is dedicated to promoting worship and services according to the faith, doctrine, and canon law of the Orthodox Tewa·∏•do Church.

As an association, we provide a spiritual home and organizational support to maintain Orthodox traditions and worship. We offer religious education, social gatherings, and moral and pastoral support for the benefit of our members and the wider community in Western Australia.

Our Ecclesiastical Position

Saint Michael Eritrean Orthodox Tewa·∏•do Church in Western Australia stands in fidelity to the canonical Orthodox tradition of the Eritrean Orthodox Tewa·∏•do Church.

In matters of ecclesiastical integrity and conscience, we openly recognize His Holiness Patriarch Abune Antonios as the rightful and legitimate Patriarch of the Eritrean Orthodox Tewa·∏•do Church. We affirm that his unjust deposition and prolonged confinement, resulting from his refusal to accept state interference in church affairs, stand as a testimony to faithfulness, humility, and moral courage.

Saint Michael Eritrean Orthodox Tewa·∏•do Church in Western Australia functions within the ecclesiastical framework of the Eritrean Orthodox Tewa·∏•do Sub-Diocese of Australia and New Zealand, through which pastoral coordination and spiritual administration are exercised in the region.

As a church community, we do not accept or support actions that compromise the spiritual independence of the Church or depart from Orthodox canonical order. Guided by prayer, peace, and humility, we remain committed to Orthodox unity, truth, and the faithful transmission of the Tewa·∏•do tradition.
