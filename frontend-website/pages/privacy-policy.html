Privacy Policy

Saint Michael Eritrean Orthodox Tewahdo Church in Western Australia Inc

Effective Date: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide to us when you:

  • Register for church membership
  • Update your member details
  • Participate in church activities and programs
  • Contact us through our website or other means

Types of information collected:

  • Personal details (name, address, phone, email, date of birth)
  • Emergency contact information
  • Church participation and membership status
  • Communication preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for:

  • Maintaining accurate church membership records
  • Communicating church news, events, and announcements
  • Providing pastoral care and spiritual support
  • Organizing church activities and programs
  • Emergency contact purposes
  • Compliance with legal and regulatory requirements

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only:

  • With your explicit consent
  • For emergency situations involving your safety
  • When required by law or legal process
  • With trusted service providers who assist in church operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Secure data storage and transmission
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments
  • Staff training on data protection

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information held by the church
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Update your communication preferences
  • Request removal from our mailing lists
  • Lodge a complaint about our privacy practices

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as:

  • You remain an active member of our church community
  • Required for legitimate church purposes
  • Required by law or regulation

Inactive member records may be archived or deleted after a reasonable period.

7. Children's Privacy

We take special care with information about children under 18:

  • Parental consent is required for children's participation in programs
  • Children's information is used only for church activities and pastoral care
  • Parents can access and update their children's information

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be:

  • Posted on our website
  • Communicated to members through our usual channels
  • Effective from the date specified in the updated policy
