Membership Declaration and Agreement

Saint Michael Eritrean Orthodox Tewahedo Church

Personal Information Declaration

I humbly declare that:

  • All information provided is true and accurate to the best of my knowledge
  • I understand that this information will be used for church membership records, pastoral care, and church communication
  • I agree to notify the church office of any changes to my contact details
  • I consent to receiving church communications, announcements, and event notifications
  • I have read and agree to the church's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service

Orthodox Tewahedo Church Membership Commitment

With understanding and reverence, I declare that:

  • I am baptized in the Orthodox Tewahedo faith, or I commit to receiving Orthodox baptism according to the tradition of the Church
  • I accept, respect, and follow the faith, doctrines, sacraments, and traditions of the Eritrean Orthodox Tewahedo Church
  • I commit to participating in the spiritual life, worship, and sacramental life of the Church as I am able
  • I understand the spiritual responsibilities and privileges of Orthodox Church membership
  • I agree to support the Church's mission, ministries, and community life according to my ability
  • I will strive to conduct myself in accordance with Orthodox Christian values, humility, repentance, and love

Community Commitment

As a member of Saint Michael Eritrean Orthodox Tewahedo Church, I commit to:

  • Being an active and faithful member of the church community
  • Respecting the clergy, church servants, and fellow members
  • Contributing positively to church fellowship, unity, and peace
  • Understanding that Orthodox Church membership involves both spiritual devotion and community responsibility

Final Confirmation

I have read, understood, and humbly agree to the above declaration.

I affirm my faith in the Orthodox Tewahedo Church and my sincere desire to join Saint Michael Eritrean Orthodox Tewahedo Church, committing myself to its spiritual life, teachings, and community in accordance with the traditions of the Church.
